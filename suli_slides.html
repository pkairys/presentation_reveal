Are perovskite alloys entropy stabilized?

Halide perovskites

  • ABX3 structure
    • A : MA, FA, Cs, Rb
    • B : Pb, Sn
    • X : I, Cl, Br
  • Trends suggest that alloying increases the stability* of certain systems, specifically when alloying on the A site.

Important Observations

  1. Bulk phase transitions (cubic --> other)
  2. Phase segregation
    • Non homogeneous phase separation
    • (FA,Cs)PbI3 --> FAPbI3(cubic) + CsPbI3(non-cubic)
    • (MA,FA,Cs)PbI3 --> slightly improves
    • (MA,FA,Cs,Rb)PbI3 --> improves further
  3. Decomposition
    • MAPbI3 --> [MA]+ + [I]- + PbI2
    • (MA,FA)PbI3 --> improves
